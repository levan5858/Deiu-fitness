Biography

Health is Wealth — the through-line of Andrei’s life.

From Romania to the UK

Originally from Romania, Andrei moved to the United Kingdom with his family. At age 17, he began his fitness journey, transforming from facing weight issues and bullying to becoming a dedicated athlete.

Through disciplined training, nutritional precision, and unwavering consistency, he developed the system that now powers Deiu Fitness coaching.

The Deiu Fitness standard

Deiu Fitness centers on high‑level training, real accountability, and consistent execution. Andrei’s methods are built from professional competition experience and adapted for everyday goals.

Execute with intent, recover with care, and keep the standard. Health is Wealth.
